Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services.

What is your certification process for pre-owned devices?

Every pre-owned device undergoes a comprehensive 50-point inspection process that includes hardware testing, software verification, battery health assessment, and cosmetic evaluation. Only devices that pass all criteria receive our certification and warranty coverage.

What warranty do you offer on your devices?

We offer a standard 12-month warranty on all certified pre-owned devices and manufacturer warranty on brand new devices. Our warranty covers hardware defects and includes technical support and repair services.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary by location: Cape Town area (1-2 business days), major cities (2-3 business days), and remote areas (3-5 business days). All orders include real-time tracking and insurance coverage.

Can I trade in my old device?

Yes! Our trade-in program allows you to get fair value for your old devices. Simply provide details about your device condition, and we'll provide a quote. Trade-in credit can be applied to your new purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, EFT transfers, and mobile payment options. We also offer flexible payment plans for qualified customers through our financing partners.

Do you offer technical support after purchase?

Absolutely! Our technical support team is available to help with setup, troubleshooting, and any questions about your device. Support is included with all purchases and can be accessed via phone, email, or live chat.
