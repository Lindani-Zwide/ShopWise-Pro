About ShopWisePro

We're passionate about connecting South Africans with premium electronics at unbeatable prices, backed by certified quality and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, ShopWisePro emerged from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to premium electronics without the premium price tag. What started as a small operation in Cape Town has grown into South Africa's most trusted destination for certified pre-owned and brand new devices.

Our journey began when we noticed a gap in the market - customers wanted high-quality devices but struggled with the high costs and uncertainty of buying pre-owned electronics. We solved this by creating a rigorous certification process that gives every customer peace of mind.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers across South Africa, from students getting their first smartphone to professionals upgrading their work devices.
